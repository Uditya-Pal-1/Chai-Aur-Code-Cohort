## Challenge 8: Simple Shopping Cart 🛒 ### Product Display - Display at least four products, each with: - Product name - Price - An "Add to Cart" button - Each product should include a simple image (you can use placeholder images). ### Cart Functionality - Users should be able to: - Add items to the cart. - Remove items from the cart. - Adjust item quantities (increase/decrease). - The cart should display: - A list of added items with their quantities. - Individual item subtotals (price × quantity). - The total cost of all items in the cart.
